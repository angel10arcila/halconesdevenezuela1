HALCONES DE VENEZUELA
Description

The Hacalcones of Venezuela is a baseball team, which is based on the values and principles of the players, focused on the sports, educational and professional development and growth of each member that makes it up, with a competitive and winning spirit.
Idea

Our idea is ambitious, since the HALCONES DE VENEZUELA is designed with the sole purpose of being a professional baseball team that belongs to the Venezuelan professional baseball league.

Perhaps for many people, it seems like it is a utopia, a dream that will never materialize, but for us, it is something that can be feasible, as long as there are people who are willing to do it.

One of the ways to achieve this is with the support of the MLB, and the teams that make it up. Creating an alliance, to found, not just one, but two baseball teams, in our league, so that it grows.

The advantages of the MLB teams investing in having a professional baseball team, in our country and in this winter league, is that it would serve them not only as a farm, but also as a team to refine the players, They are aiming to reach the big leagues, before starting the "Springs Trainers." When these players arrive, let them participate in these teams in optimal conditions.

Another way to consolidate, this is with the union of ex-major league players, businessmen or foreign investors, who merge these two new teams into the LVBP. Over time, I have heard that several players have tried to buy a professional baseball team, and now, none of them have been able to materialize that dream, of having a team already formed in our league. Because? No, it is better to found two new teams, and start a new story within our baseball.
